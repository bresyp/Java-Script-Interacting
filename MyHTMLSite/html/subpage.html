How to Make Chocolate Chip Cookies
  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Mix the sugars, butter, vanilla extract and egg into large bowl
  3. Add in flour, baking soda and salt
  4. Add the chocolate chips
  5. Butter the cookie sheet
  6. Place round tablespoons of cookie dough on the sheets
  7. Place in oven and bake for 10 minutes
